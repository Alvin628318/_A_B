Your goal is to guess a valid 4-digit number that is randomly generated.
A valid 4-digit number is one that does not have repeating digits.
For example, "3476" and "0291" is valid while "7550" is not.
Every time you guess a valid 4-digit number, you will be hinted with the number of correct digits you guessed;
For every digit that exists within the answer and is located at the same spot, you get 1 A.
For every digit that exists within the answer but is not located at the same spot, you get 1 B.
For example, if the answer were to be "1234", guessing "2574" will hint you with "1A1B"; guessing 9705 will hint you with "0A0B".
Your goal is to use the hints given to you every time you guess a valid 4-digit number to infer and guess the answer.
If you do so, you will see a "4A0B". This means that you have correctly guessed the randomly generated number.
You can continue on guessing, and your results will be shown on the left every time you sucessfully guessed an answer.
Have fun!
